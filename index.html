Methodology in Action: TechCorp Wellness Transformation

See how our systematic approach delivered measurable results for a Fortune 500 technology company's comprehensive wellness program implementation.

1. Project Charter

Challenge: Low employee engagement, rising healthcare costs

Our Approach: Comprehensive stakeholder analysis identified 12 key stakeholder groups, established clear ROI targets of 3:1 return, and created risk mitigation strategies for low participation.

2. Strategic Roadmap

Solution: 8-month phased implementation with pilot program

Our Approach: Visual timeline with weekly communication cadence, monthly stakeholder reviews, and quarterly executive briefings ensured alignment throughout.

3. Agile Execution

Implementation: Sprint-based rollout across 5,000 employees

Our Approach: 2-week sprints with daily standups, continuous user feedback integration, and adaptive planning based on real-time participation data.

4. Performance Tracking

Results: 94% participation, $2.4M annual savings, 67% health risk reduction

Our Approach: Real-time dashboard monitoring enabled rapid course corrections and demonstrated clear ROI to leadership throughout the project.
